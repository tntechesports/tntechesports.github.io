About the Club

The esports club of Tennessee Technological University is a multifunctional club thats scope ranges from a casual gaming club to a competitive collegiate esports brand. Our casual gaming club is meant to connect Tech students from all disciplines who have a love for video games and want to find like-minded individuals. The casual side of our club is geared toward being a fun and friendly environment for our club members to meet new people and make lasting relationships. The competitive collegiate esports brand of our club is geared to finding the best gaming talent at Tech and form esports teams that compete in various tournaments across the nation with prizing usually being thousands of dollars in scholarships. We currently have roughly 60 players in varying degrees competing in 6 games that compete in events like Tespaâ€™s Collegiate Overwatch and Collegiate Rocket League tournaments for scholarship prizing ranging from $10-$75K. Our collegiate teams have ranked in the top 16 in the nation, as well as some taking 1st place at some live tournaments like VOLAN at UTK. The future of collegiate esports at Tennessee Tech will be to continue to grow and prosper as we draw in better players and coaches and work with the university to offer a varsity esports program with scholarships to entice the best players to come to Tech as well as a dedicated esports arena for our players to practice together on good equipment.
